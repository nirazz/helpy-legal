What NOT to Track

🚫 Vanity Metrics - Ignore These
📱 Total downloads
👁️ Pageviews / screen views
⏱️ Session length
📣 Social media followers
⭐ App Store rating average

These feel good but don't tell you whether the product works. A user spending 30 minutes in the app could mean they're engaged or that they're lost. Measure actions that create value - offers made, matches created, help delivered.
