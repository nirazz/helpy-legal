How to Delete Your Account

You can delete your account directly from the app, or request deletion via email:

  1. Send an email to support@help-y.com with the subject "Account Deletion Request"
  2. Include the email address associated with your Helpy account
  3. We will process your request within 7 days
  4. You will receive a confirmation email once your account has been deleted
Request Account Deletion

What Data Is Deleted

When your account is deleted, the following data will be permanently removed:

  • Profile information - Your name, email, profile photo, and location
  • Help requests - All requests you have posted
  • Chat messages - All messages you have sent and received
  • Matches - All help matches you were part of
  • Ratings - All ratings you have given and received
  • Push notification tokens - Your device push tokens

Data Retention

All data is deleted immediately upon processing your request. No data is retained after account deletion.

Questions?

If you have questions, contact us at support@help-y.com.
