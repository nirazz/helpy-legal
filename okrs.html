What are OKRs?

Objectives = where we're going (qualitative, inspiring). Key Results = how we know we got there (quantitative, measurable). Graded 0.0 to 1.0. The sweet spot is 0.7 - consistently hitting 1.0 means you're not being ambitious enough. OKRs are a direction-setting tool, not a performance review.
