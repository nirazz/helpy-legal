Browse

Discover Nearby Requests

Browse help requests from your neighbors as swipe cards or on a live map. Each card shows photos, distance, urgency, and the requester's profile.

  • Pill buttons: "→ Skip" and "✋ Offer Help" (no dating-app hearts)
  • Cards/Map toggle — tap any pin to jump to that request
  • PostGIS distance filtering up to 10km radius
  • 12 categories: Elderly Care, Moving, Tech Help, Tutoring, and more
  • 3 urgency levels with color-coded indicators
  • Pinch-to-zoom image viewer (up to 4×) on request photos
  • Skeleton loading placeholders while fetching
Post

Ask for Help in Seconds

Create a help request with a title, description, category, and urgency. Preview your card before posting, and attach up to 3 photos from camera or gallery.

  • 12-category grid picker with emoji icons
  • Post Preview — see exactly how your card looks before submitting
  • Up to 3 photo uploads (camera or gallery, quality-compressed)
  • EN + HE content filter blocks inappropriate language
  • Phone verification required before first post
Map

Browse by Map

Switch to Map view to see nearby requests as pins on an interactive map. Tap any pin to preview the request, then skip or offer help right from the bottom sheet.

  • Cards / Map toggle on Browse screen
  • Category emoji pins — colored by urgency
  • Selected pin zooms in with animated bottom sheet
  • Skip or Offer Help without leaving the map
  • PostGIS-powered distance sorting
Manage

Track Your Requests

View all your active and past requests. Accept offers with a celebration animation, decline others, close when done, and rate your helper — all in one place.

  • Active and History tabs
  • Accept or decline helper offers
  • Celebration animation 🎉 on accept + "Chat Now" CTA
  • Close request → automatic rating prompt
  • View request photos with pinch-to-zoom
Profile

Your Community Profile

Track your community impact, set your search radius, manage blocked users, and toggle between English and Hebrew with full RTL support.

  • Community stats: Helped, Got Help, ⭐ Rating, Posted
  • Verification badge for Google/Apple sign-in users
  • Edit name & photo with iOS-style ActionSheet + EDIT overlay
  • Blocked Users list with one-tap Unblock
  • Account deletion and support contact

🇮🇱 Hebrew

Full RTL Support

Every screen, button, and label is translated. The entire layout flips to right-to-left for Hebrew speakers. Distances automatically switch to kilometers.

  • One-tap language toggle on Profile screen
  • All 5 tabs + headers fully translated
  • Navigation arrows flip for RTL (‹ → ›)
  • Hebrew uses km (ק״מ) instead of miles
  • Preference synced to Supabase across devices
