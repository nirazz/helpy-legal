Vision

"A world where no neighbor feels alone, and no request for help goes unanswered."

Mission

"Make it effortless for neighbors to help each other with everyday tasks - free, trusted, and human."
The Problem We're Solving

Cities are getting lonelier. In Tel Aviv, you can live next to someone for 5 years and not know their name. Yet that neighbor has a drill you need, time you could use, a skill that solves your problem in 20 minutes.

The gap between "I need help" and "my neighbor can help" isn't a capability gap. It's a social friction gap. People want to connect and help each other - they just need a low-stakes, judgment-free way to ask.

Traditional solutions fail: Facebook groups are noisy and feel exposed. WhatsApp threads die. Nextdoor became toxic. TaskRabbit costs money and removes the human element entirely.

Helpy removes the friction. Not with money. Not with contracts. With a swipe.

TAM - Total Addressable Market
$15B
Global neighborhood and community help market. 4.5B urban dwellers globally. If 10% would use a neighborhood help app, that's 450M potential users at ~$33 annual value each.
SAM - Serviceable Addressable Market
2.7M
Israel + Hebrew diaspora (~10M). Tel Aviv metro is the densest urban cluster - ~3M residents. 90% smartphone penetration = 2.7M addressable users who could realistically download and use Helpy.
SOM - Serviceable Obtainable Market
5,000
Year 1 target: Tel Aviv only. Target 1% of 500K core Tel Aviv residents = 5,000 MAU. Achievable through neighborhood ambassador program and organic word-of-mouth.
Business Model Options
🌐
CURRENT

Forever Free

Zero cost, zero friction. Build trust and community goodwill first.

+Maximum viral growth
+Community trust
-Zero revenue
-Unsustainable solo
⚡

Freemium Boost

Free base. Pay to "Boost" a request to top of the local feed for 24h.

+Low friction paywall
+Aligns value + price
-Pay-to-win optics
-Needs volume first
🏆

Helper Subscription

~$5/mo for power helpers. Priority in the feed, verified badge, advanced filters.

+Recurring revenue
+Rewards best helpers
-Needs helper tiers first
-Feels weird for free help
🏛️

Municipality B2B

Cities pay for community programs. White-label Helpy for neighborhoods.

+Large contract value
+Seeding the market
-Long sales cycles
-Needs social proof first
