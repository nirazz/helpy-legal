Browse

The right kind of scrolling.

Swipe through what your neighbors need. Category, distance, urgency, photos — all at a glance. Tap to offer help.

  • Offer help in one tap
  • Loads what's near you, fast
  • Hides anyone you've blocked or reported
Map view

See your neighborhood.

Switch to the map to see what's needed where. Pins are color-coded by category. Tap any pin — the request opens right below.

  • Color-coded pins by category
  • Map and card stay in sync
  • Toggle back to cards anytime
Post

Ask for help in seconds.

Pick a category, write a sentence, snap a photo. That's the whole thing. Your post lands in front of nearby neighbors right away.

  • 12 categories — from groceries to repairs
  • Up to 3 photos from camera or gallery
  • Mark how urgent it is
Chat

Coordinate in real time.

Once you match, chat to figure out the where, when, and how. Messages are instant — no refresh, no delay.

  • Typing indicators
  • Read receipts
  • Mark complete & rate when you're done
Activity

Everything in one place.

All your requests and offers, active and past. Accept or decline with a tap. Tap any photo to zoom in. Rate your helper when the job's done.

  • Active and history tabs
  • Accept or decline offers in a tap
  • Quick path to chat after accepting
Profile

Build a community reputation.

A real public profile with your rating, reviews, and the help you've given. Streaks and a monthly leaderboard make showing up feel good.

  • Helped, got help, rating, posted
  • Daily streak counter
  • Monthly neighborhood leaderboard
Hebrew & English

One app, two languages.

Full Hebrew with right-to-left layout, full English. One tap to switch — the app remembers.

Trust & safety

Built safe, from day one.

Safety isn't an extra. It's part of how Helpy is designed — at the surface and underneath.

Verified phones

Every helper verifies their phone before offering help. No anonymous offers.

Report & block

One tap to remove anyone from your view. Blocking is instant and goes both ways.

Private by default

Your exact location is never shared. Helpers see distance, not your address.

Delete anytime

Erase your account and every trace of your data with one tap. No questions, no waiting.
